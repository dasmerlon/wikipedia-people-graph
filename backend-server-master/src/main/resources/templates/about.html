About the project

This project was formed in context of a practical training by the base.camp at the University of Hamburg.
The main aim of the practical training was to handle, analyse and visualize Big Data.
The team of "Wikipedia People Graph" consists of four computer science students, who want to make Wikipedia's information more visual and usable.

We were especially interested in the personal information of Wikipedia. Therefore, the goal of this project was to create an easy access to the personal data of Wikipedia.
We want users to be able to easily explore the people of wikipedia and their relationships. For this purpose we created a timeline and a network graph.
The data we used is based on several Hadoop MapReduce jobs that allowed us to analyse Wikimedia dump files.

We would like to thank the base.camp, who kindly provided us their Hadoop cluster.
