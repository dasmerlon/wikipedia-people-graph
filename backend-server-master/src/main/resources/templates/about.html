About the project

Our team and this project was formed in context of a practical training by the base.camp at the University of Hamburg.
The "Wikipedia People Graph"-team consists of 4 computer science students, who want to make the wealth of Wikipedia's information more visual and usable.

We are especially interested in the personal data of Wikipedia. The goal of this project is to create an easy entry to Wikipedias's personal data. We want our users to be able to easily explore the persons of wikipedia and their relations. For this purpose we built the timeline and the network graph. The data we use is based on several Hadoop MapReduce Jobs, by which we were able to analyse Wikimedia dump files.

We would like to thank the base.camp of University of Hamburg, who kindly provided us their Hadoop cluster. We also would like to thank Eugen Ruppert from the base.camp team for his continuous and very helpful support during the project.
